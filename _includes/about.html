About

Less than 4% of Africa's collections are available digitally. The Hidden Collections Africa initiative is a proposed new grant program to catalog and digitize materials at institutions in Africa that are inaccessible or at risk of being lost forever. The initiative draws inspiration from CLIR's large-scale grant programs and directly supports institutions and staff across the African continent to meet urgent preservation needs, close gaps in the historical record, and make records more accessible to users.
