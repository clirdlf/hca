Hidden Collections
AFRICA

Decolonizing knowledge spaces through digitizing African special collections and archives.

Source: World Population Prospects 2022

The Challenge

The Universal Declaration of Human Rights asserts that access to information is a universal human right, yet globally, Africa has the lowest number of accessible and available collections, both digital and print.

Of collections available online worldwide, materials held by African collecting organisations make up no more than 4% of the total available digital content while the 54 nations of the African continent comprise over 17% of the global population.
